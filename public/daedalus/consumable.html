Engineering and Manufacturing of plastic disposables for the Radiopharmaceutical, Pharmaceutical, and Medical industry…

Our broad experience in developing and manufacturing a wide variety of medical and diagnostic devices, has made Daedalus Engineering a leading solution provider for the healthcare market. We can handle complex projects and combine our technical and manufacturing expertise to create versatile customized solutions.

As a full-range service provider, Daedalus Engineering has the capability to take your concept from initial design to the supply of high quality ready to market products. Customer can take benefit of our extensive experience, professionalism and innovative approach.

 

With a full regulatory compliance and controlled manufacturing environments: EN ISO 9001:2008 & EN ISO 13485:2012 + AC:2012, Daedalus Engineering provides clean-room assembly in Class D, ultrasonic welding, bonding, cleaning, finishing operations and packaging process. This capability completes the production chain for our customer’s products, and allows Daedalus Engineering to be a single source.

 

We are capable of handling current production programs as well as managing the entire process from prototype through production.
